At its heart lies the subtle spice of **coriander** , resting on a smooth, lasting base of **musk** and **cedar** , creating a calm yet confident scent that speaks softly but leaves a lasting impression.

### Frequently asked questions

What is the longevity of your perfumes? 

Our perfumes are crafted with high-quality ingredients and typically last 8 to 12 hours, depending on skin type and weather conditions.  
  


When will I get my order? 

We will work quickly to ship your order as soon as possible. But its takes 4 to 5 days. Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with further information. 

Do you offer gift wrapping or special packaging? 

Yes! We offer elegant gift wrapping and handwritten note options at checkout — perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, or special occasions.  
  


What ingredients are used in your perfumes? 

We use high-quality essential oils, natural extracts, and skin-safe synthetics. Full ingredient lists are available on each product page.
